ZodyTrack — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 5 July 2026

ZodyTrack (“the app”) is a TV & movie tracking app. This policy explains what information the app handles. In short: ZodyTrack does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. Your tracking data stays on your device.

Data stored on your device

The shows and movies you track, the episodes and movies you mark as watched, your watch history and statistics, and your app settings (such as language) are stored locally on your device only, in the app’s private storage. This information is never sent to us or to any server. We have no account system and no access to this data.

Backups

You can optionally export your data to a backup file and choose where to save it (for example your own cloud drive or device storage). This action is entirely under your control. ZodyTrack does not upload or access your backups — where the file goes is determined by you and the service you choose to save it to.

Third-party content (TMDB)

The app fetches show and movie information (titles, images, descriptions, episode lists, ratings and availability) from The Movie Database (TMDB). These are read-only requests for public catalogue information. This product uses the TMDB API but is not endorsed or certified by TMDB. Please review TMDB’s own privacy policy for how they handle API requests. Streaming availability is provided via JustWatch.

Advertising

The current version of ZodyTrack does not show ads and does not initialize any advertising services, and therefore does not collect an advertising identifier. If advertising is introduced in a future version, this policy will be updated beforehand to describe it.

Children

ZodyTrack does not knowingly collect any personal information from anyone, including children.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the “last updated” date above will change and the new version will be published at this address.
